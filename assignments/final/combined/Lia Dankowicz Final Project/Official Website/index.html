Welcome! This site depicts a genealogy of the extended Swedish royal family, from members of the monarchy in the mid-10th century to the family of the current king, Carl XVI Gustaf.

The Pages of the Site

The About the Project page contains a description of the reasoning behind and process of creating this genealogy, the limitations of the software in use, known as Gramps.

The full contents of the genealogy can be viewed on the Individuals and Surnames pages, while links to closer examinations of the Swedish monarchy can be found on the Royal Family page.

The Media page shows a spread of photos and paintings portraying members of the extended royal family throughout the years. These photos can also be found on the individual family members' pages.

A summary of the data represented on the site can be found on this Records page.
